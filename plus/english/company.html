Company


PLUS Career founded in 2007. We aim to provide professional education to different talents and help students search their career. We offer career consulting service, increasing students’ competitiveness. We already helped thousands of students succeed in their job searching in the US. Now we have several different branch offices in several big cities, including New York, Washington D.C., and Los Angeles, etc.

PLUS Career also aim to help employers succeed in finding right candidates. We have sufficient candidate pool every year and we welcome any corporations to join us!
